A powerful factor for the rapid growth of stock indices

Today, the stock market is a powerful "inciter" and it is UBER that puts the ticker "in the bonfire".Or rather, the stock market itself puts the ticker "in the bonfire" - this is how the Nikkei stock index (the so-called" black market " index) reacts to its every move.In the 11 years since the beginning of the crisis, stock indices in America and Europe have grown significantly.But in the Asian and American ones, they have grown much more slowly. Why is this happening? And more importantly, how exactly should I buy index funds now? In this article, we will talk about the main factors that generate an increase in stock indices (and how to buy them):1) Price tag. We are talking about the face value of the index funds you bought. This is the only factor that can significantly affect their growth. In particular, it can cause a drop in the stock market and a rise in the indices. In March, we saw a drop in the index (the Shanghai index) and a rise (the Shenzhen index). 2) Fiscal stimulus. This is probably the most powerful factor for the rapid growth of stock indices.It it reduces the risks of losing the invested money (by reducing interest rates on loans and by providing liquidity to financial instruments). In March, we saw a significant drop in stock indices in both the United States and China.This was due to the slowing economy in both countries (which is already facing a severe winter). a severe drop in oil demand and a rise in stock prices of oil-producing companies (the Shanghai index declined by 11% and 20%, respectively). 3) " Fountain of youth"This word describes the powerful effect that has been created by the arrival of the first week of new year's holidays.When people start working and saving money, new year's New Year's resolutions are born. This is also the period of greatest uncertainty for the economy and for each of us.We see first-hand how the effects of the virus are being felt in the workplace.Everyone from airline pilots and engineers to janitors and waiters feel the consequences of the severe restrictions imposed on them.4) Arbitrage. Now the stock market has reached a historic high. And it is time to start buying shares.Because this is the only way to protect your invested money from overheating (and from a possible collapse in the market due to a collapse in financial markets).In the dating market, only those who can realistically bet on the continuation of the trends of the stock market will be successful.And only those who can actively trade on the stock market (i.e., those who actively buy and sell securities):This also includes those who invest their money in index funds (by means of a long-term investment strategy
